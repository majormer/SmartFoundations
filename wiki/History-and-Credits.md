# History and Credits

Smart! has a long history in the Satisfactory modding community.

## Short History

Smart! began as an original building-assist mod by Alex. It became one of the defining factory-building mods for Satisfactory because it made large repeated builds much easier: grids, manifolds, scaling, extending, and precision placement all became part of the Smart! identity.

The Unreal Engine 5 transition for Satisfactory broke many older mod systems, including the original Smart! codebase. The current Smart! rebuild for Satisfactory 1.1+ is maintained by Finalomega with guidance and permission from Alex.

The rebuilt Smart! focuses on the same core idea: help players build faster while staying vanilla-friendly. It still places standard Satisfactory buildables and charges normal material costs.

Smart! source became publicly available for transparency, review, and contributions on April 26, 2026.
