# Smart! Wiki

Smart! is a building-assist mod for Satisfactory. It helps place repeated factory layouts faster while still using vanilla buildables, normal material costs, and save-safe construction.

Smart! does not add free buildings, free resources, or custom production machines. Its scope is factory building and efficiency, primarily while the build gun is equipped.
