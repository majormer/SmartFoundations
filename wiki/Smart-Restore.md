# Smart Restore

Smart Restore saves your Smart! setups as presets so you can reuse them later, share them with other players, or rebuild a whole factory module the same way every time.

It is the answer to "I spent ages dialing in this layout — how do I get it back?"

> Screenshot placeholder: Smart Panel with the Presets list open.

## What A Preset Saves

A preset can capture:

- Grid size (X, Y, Z).
- Spacing, Steps, Stagger, and Rotation.
- The selected production recipe.
- Auto-Connect settings.
- A restored Extend topology — a whole factory-module layout, not just the numbers.

## Using Presets

Open the Smart Panel with `K` and use the `Presets >>` button to:

- **Save** your current setup as a named preset.
- **Apply** a saved preset later to set the panel back to it.
- **Export / Import** a preset to share it, or to load one someone shared with you.
- **Capture from Last Extend** — turn the last Extend layout you built into a reusable preset.

## Import From Last Extend

After you build with Extend, Smart Restore can capture that exact layout as an editable preset draft — the source building, its recipe where available, the factories, belts, conveyor lifts, pipes, distributors, power poles, and the cloned connection layout. It is the fastest way to turn a module you just built by hand into something you can replay anywhere.

## Sharing Is Progression-Safe

Shared presets are checked against your current unlocks before they can be imported or applied. If a preset needs a building, recipe, belt or pipe tier, lift, splitter, merger, power component, or other piece you have not unlocked yet, Smart! rejects it instead of letting you build something you could not place by hand.

That keeps shared presets honest: importing one never skips progression.
