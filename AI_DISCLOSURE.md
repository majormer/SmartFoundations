# AI-Assisted Development Disclosure

Smart! uses AI-assisted development. This disclosure explains what that means for the project.

## What AI assistance is used for

AI tools are used extensively across this project, including:

- **C++ implementation** — drafting new features, refactoring existing code, and debugging.
- **Architecture and design** — working through design decisions, service decomposition, and API exploration.
- **Documentation** — writing, editing, and organizing docs, changelogs, and this repository's policy files.
- **Investigation and research** — analyzing Unreal Engine and Satisfactory APIs, reproducing bugs, and tracing code paths.
- **Testing and triage** — identifying failure modes, writing diagnostic logging, and interpreting crash output.

## What AI assistance does not do

- AI does not make final decisions about features, design, or release.
- AI does not test the mod in-game. All in-game validation is done by the maintainer and community testers.
- AI does not have authority over the project scope or roadmap. See [GOVERNANCE.md](GOVERNANCE.md).
- AI-generated code is reviewed, tested, and owned by the maintainer before release.

## Why this is disclosed

Transparency about AI use is important. Some contributors and users want to know how software they rely on or contribute to is built. This disclosure exists so that information is available and honest.

AI assistance does not reduce the accountability of the maintainer. Bugs, design decisions, and releases are the responsibility of Finalomega, not the AI tools used during development.

## Community testing still matters

Smart! features are validated through developer review, in-game testing, Discord tester feedback, and bug reports. AI-assisted development does not replace that. If you find a bug or unexpected behavior, please [report it](https://github.com/majormer/SmartFoundations/issues).
